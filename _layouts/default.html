About Me

I am an MSc student in AI at the University of Amsterdam. During my program, I developed a passion for research and have published several papers on topics including conformal prediction, information retrieval, and the intersection of hyperbolic learning and multimedia analytics.

I am currently working on my thesis, which focuses on developing general-purpose robotic world models capable of solving long-horizon tasks. I am excited to have started this project and to engage deeply with the topic over an extended period. I am supervised by Dr. Andrii Zadaianchuk and Prof. Efstratios Gavves.

Current Activities
  • Thesis: I have started my thesis under the supervision of Dr. Andrii Zadaianchuk and Prof. Efstratios Gavves.
  • Freelance: AI Software Developer at LeerLevels.
  • Research Internship: Working on a physics benchmark for video generation models. First preprint available here.
